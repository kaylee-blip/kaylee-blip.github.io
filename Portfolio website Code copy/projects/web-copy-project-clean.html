Website Copywriting for Business and Marketing Agency

My Role

Website Content Strategist & Copywriter

I served as the lead copywriter, responsible for developing all written content across 21 service subpages of a full-scale business and marketing agency website.

The Problem

The client needed to clearly communicate the value of their wide range of services to potential customers. The client's previous website lacked clear, well-structured messaging, which made it difficult for potential customers to understand exactly what services the agency offered.

Skills & Expertise Applied

  • Copywriting for web and service-based content
  • Adapting copy to an established brand voice
  • SEO-conscious content writing
  • Proofreading and editing for clarity and consistency
  • Ensuring consistency across a large content set

Why it Mattered

Clarifying the agency's services and benefits made it easy for prospects to find the right solution and understand value quickly. Consistent, benefit-focused messaging strengthened credibility, improved SEO discoverability across 21 service subpages, and aligned calls-to-action to drive more qualified leads.

The following excerpts are samples of website copy I wrote for Sopra Solutions. These passages are taken directly from the live website.
