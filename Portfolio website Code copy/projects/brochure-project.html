Brochure Copywriting & Design for Agency Sales Team

My Role

Copywriter and Designer
I designed a suite of brochures for a marketing and business operations agency, creating tailored collateral that clearly communicated their range of services.

The Problem

The client lacked adequate assets and materials for their sales team. Without professional collateral, prospects had nothing to review after meetings, eliminating any consistent touchpoint once conversations ended. New brochures needed to showcase the agency's unique value propositions and service offerings in a visually appealing format that aligned with their brand identity.

Skills & Expertise Applied

  • Brochure design and layout with Canva
  • Information hierarchy and content structuring
  • Proofreading and editing service descriptions
  • Applying and maintaining consistency with an established brand identity

Deliverables

  • Two-page brochure highlighting the agency's business services
  • Two-page brochure outlining the agency's creative services
  • One-page brochure presenting the agency's executive coaching services
